ABOUT ME

Hey how's it going? As you might be able to tell, I'm Marshall. I specialize in using JavaScipt to make the web a more enjoyable and useful place for everyone.

I've been using computers since I can remember: dial-up internet and good ole' AOL. Despite my many interests from music to fitness, technology has been a constant. I love tinkering with things until I figure them out and using my skills to solve all kinds of problems.

I have a pretty eclectic background of experience. I attended Indiana Wesleyan University and graduated with a bachelor's in Computer Information Systems. Before I knew my focus, I started out interning as a IT technician and then transitioned into marketing. After getting my feet wet, I knew I wanted to specialize in web development.
